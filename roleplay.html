Opening Analysis Close

Example Roleplay Flow

1. Opening (20-30 sec)

"Thank you for meeting with me today. I analyzed your current challenge, and I prepared a strategy focused on improving customer retention and profitability over the next quarter."

2. Situation Analysis

"Your current decline appears tied to inconsistent follow-up and low repeat purchase behavior. That creates acquisition pressure and lowers marketing efficiency."

3. Strategy Recommendation

"I recommend a loyalty campaign with segmented offers, paired with automated follow-up messaging to increase retention and average order value."

4. KPI Measurement

"We will track repeat purchase rate, customer lifetime value, and campaign conversion so we can optimize quickly."

5. Risk Handling

"To control risk, we can launch a small pilot first, compare outcomes against a control group, then scale once performance is validated."

6. Close

"With your approval, we can begin implementation this week and review the first KPI dashboard in two weeks."

What Judges Notice Fast

Executive Presence

Clear tone, confident posture, and concise transitions.

Business Logic

Recommendations tied to measurable outcomes.

Scenario Accuracy

Specific references to the roleplay details, not generic advice.
